What is toki pona reading helper?

This tool generates toki pona text tagged with tooltips with dictionary info.

This webpage features valid HTML5 and (almost) valid CSS (except for the tooltip plugin.)

Contact

This is an opensource project, freely available at tokipona-helper's GitHub repository.

Find me as kaoD/jan Awalo in #tokipona @ FreeNode.

Collaborating

Currently, dictionaries are the top priority.

I'm (slowly) working in adapting jan Pije's public domain lessons but I could use some help.

Alternate dictionaries are very welcome: old toki pona, new toki pona, with banned words, other destination languages, etc.

Suggestions, ideas, new meanings, but reports, etc. are VERY welcome!

You can also submit your texts to be included in the preset texts.

Help

Basic usage

Just type in the textbox and toki pona text will appear below it, just like this:

mi sona tan ni: sina lukin e sitelen mi

Words are tagged with dictionary tooltips. Keep your mouse over any word to show its meaning.

Unknown/invalid words are marked in deep red.

flux capacitor

Foreign (capitalized) words are marked in deep green.

jan Awalo tan ma Epanja

Dictionaries

Select any dictionary in the list to enable it.

Look closely! Dictionary settings are saved in your browser's URL. You can even share this link!

"view" shows a handy HTML formatted version of the dictionary.

"raw" downloads the dictionary as a JSON file.

Preset texts

These are good to practice toki pona.

Selecting any item loads the text into the textarea.

"raw" downloads the text as plaintext file.

Link sharing

A special anchor titled "link" (below the textarea) will always point to your current text and dictionary.

You can bookmark or share this link to share texts and dictionary settings.

Be careful: long URLs may fail under some browsers.
